Our Story

Born from a love of Japan’s private karaoke culture. Built in downtown Fayetteville.

Big Box started with years spent in Japan, a lasting love for private-room karaoke, and a business idea that stayed alive until the right Fayetteville location finally opened the door.

A place made for the people who actually want to stay awhile.

Founders Mailena and Justin Urso fell in love with karaoke while living in Japan, carried the idea through Chicago, kept the vision alive through setbacks, and ultimately opened Big Box Karaoke in Fayetteville at the end of 2018.

2006–07
Japan planted the seed.

Living and teaching in Shikoku introduced the private karaoke experience that would shape everything later.

Chicago
The idea became a plan.

Frequent private-room karaoke nights with friends turned a favorite pastime into a real business concept.

2018
Big Box opened in Fayetteville.

The first location launched in downtown Fayetteville and quickly became a celebration spot locals return to.

Now
Still the go-to room for letting loose.

Seven private suites, full food and bar service, special events, sake, and nights that feel bigger than the calendar slot they started in.
